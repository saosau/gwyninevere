I Sometimes Feel Like a Hardstuck Plat

Tue Dec 20, 2022 · 371 words

Great, I officially burned myself out.

I’m kidding, but things got the hang of me. Either people don’t have enough time to listen, or it doesn’t get through to anyone. So I create this space where I can become mellow at least to myself. Sometimes this feeling eats me, so I tell myself I’ll work, but I finally lose to it, so I feel like this is the safest way for me to express myself.

When we (our generation) call ourselves hard stuck plat, it just really means we are in a slump. To get to platinum you need to get the hang of the game pretty well. Then there comes the grind through diamond to masters until you reach challenger and get a backpack. I stopped playing games far ago, but sometimes it feels like being hardstuck (plat).

Obviously, I’ve been sitting in front of the computer for far too long. All of my posts are on Computer Science section so it really is depressing in a way (just kidding). So I’ve been pushing myself to end all the projects early, and here I am, finally free. Honestly I got a bit sick of screens, so in a way I like the fact that when we learn theory, we code on paper.

I may not seem like it, but I obviously love the subject, I mean who doesn’t, anyone uses technology subconsciously. (Personally, I think giving kids “smart” phones is a really bad idea.) But god, please, how can you talk about your favourite algorithm for the whole day? And have literally no other things you could talk about. Maybe I’m too stupid or dull to understand how people can just study their whole life. Well, at least I know I am not the best student. And I’ll never be, but I do admire and respect the best.

So my friend recommended me this TV show for me to lash out on called “Bocchi the Rock!”. It was pretty interesting. One thing that got into my mind was when the protagonist said she was lonely so she played 6 hours every day. I thought, well I can’t say that, maybe that’s the problem.

So the songs are really, pretty decent.
